## Requirements

-   Onesync (infinity/legacy)
-   ox_lib
-   ox_target (optional)
-   rcore_doorlock / ox_doorlock

## Installation

<Steps>
### Download the newest version from [CFX Portal](https://portal.cfx.re/assets/granted-assets)

### Put ensure vzn-battering-ram in your server resources folder

### Add `ensure vzn-battering-ram` to your server.cfg

</Steps>

## Compatibility

-   Framework
