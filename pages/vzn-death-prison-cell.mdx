## Requirements

-   Onesync (infinity/legacy)
-   ox_lib
-   ox_target (optional)
-   [Wuja Death Room](https://wuja-maps.tebex.io/package/6312401)

## Installation

<Steps>
### Download the newest version from [Keymaster](https://keymaster.fivem.net/asset-grants)

### Put vzn-death-prison-cell in your server resources folder

### Add `ensure vzn-death-prison-cell` to your server.cfg

### If you are running on ESX -> [ESX Integration](./vzn-death-prison-cell/integration/esx.mdx)

</Steps>

## Compatibility
